Our Mission

Our mission is to celebrate and simplify the discovery of local coffee culture in Surabaya. We aim to be the most comprehensive and trusted guide for coffee lovers, connecting them with the unique and thriving coffee scene this city has to offer.

Our Story

Born from a shared passion for caffeine and community, Surabaya Coffee started as a small blog. We spent weekends exploring hidden gems and bustling cafes, realizing that Surabaya's coffee story deserved to be told. We've since grown into a dedicated platform, committed to showcasing the craftsmanship and character of every coffee shop in the city.

Why Surabaya?

Surabaya has a rich history with coffee, and today it's a bustling hub of innovation. From traditional 'warung kopi' to third-wave specialty shops, the city offers a diverse coffee experience unlike any other. We're here to help you navigate this exciting landscape and find your new favorite spot.
